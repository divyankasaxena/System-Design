What is System Design?
Every second:

Thousands of videos are uploaded.
Millions of videos are watched.
Millions of comments are posted.
Billions of requests reach the servers.

Yet it still works.

How?
Because its system is designed to handle huge scale.

Goal of System Design

We try to build systems that are:

Fast ⚡
Reliable ✅
Scalable 📈
Secure 🔒
Available 24×7 🌍
